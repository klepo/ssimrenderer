1 SSIM Renderer Library
2 =====================
3 SSIM Renderer is a Qt-based C++ library for OpenGL enabled rendering
4 of virtual X-Ray images from statistical shape and intensity models.
5 
6 Description
7 -----------
8 The main focus of the library is a rendering of virtual X-Ray images from
9 a statistical shape and intensity model (SSIM) proposed by R. Yao [1].
10 The library provides OpenGL accelerated rendering of the SSIM according to [2].
11 Moreover, it includes OpenGL and OpenCL implementations of two image similarity
12 metrics: joint histogram normalized mutual information metric and sum
13 of squared differences metric. The library is highly optimised and suitable
14 for purposes of 2D-3D deformable registration [3]. It is also capable to work
15 with simple polygonal statistical shape models (SSM) stored in Statismo file
16 format.
17 
18 Version from 17 December 2015.
19 
20 Features
21 --------
22  * Rendering of virtual X-Ray images from SSIM.
23  * Rendering of surface images from SSIM/SSM.
24  * Rendering of silhouettes of SSIM/SSM.
25  * Mirroring of the shape models.
26  * Sharing shape model between many renderers using OpenGL shared contexts.
27  * Exporting the surface of the shape model in STL file format.
28  * Computation of OpenGL and OpenCL accelerated image similarity metrics.
29  * etc.

31 Prerequisites
32 -------------

34 redistributed within the SSIM Renderer package. The libraries are redistributed

41  * zlib
